Highness Princess Dato' Seri Dr. Ramona Murad

Visionary Leader | Global Ambassador | Advocate
for Human Rights & Economic Development

Her Highness Princess Dato' Seri Dr. Romona Murad (SSAS, DSMP, DMP, SMP, MIPR) of Malaysia embodies multifaceted leadership and is the visionary behind Romona Consultancy and Management, headquartered in the vibrant city of Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.

In her esteemed capacity, Princess Romona serves as the Ambassador-at-Large for Malaysia under the auspices of the International Human Rights Organization (IHRO), while simultaneously presiding as the Country President for Malaysia within the Economic Council of India. Princess Romona also holds position as Regional Director for the Global Hope Foundation based in Michigan, USA.

Her chairmanship extends to the Malaysian branch of the esteemed Royal Society Group, affiliated with the global network based in Al-Jubail, Saudi Arabia. Her dedication also sees her as a Patron Member of the Euro Asia Financial Policy Forum (EAFPF), anchored in Italy. Princess Romona also performs duties as the Honorary Chairman and Vice-Sovereign of the Sovereign Council of YUDA (Youth United for Development Association) based in Cameroon, Central Africa.

Marking her influence in the international dialogue, Princess Romona was designated as Chairperson for the International Commission for Malaysia by the International Organization for Peace and International Security to Activate International Humanitarian Law for Peace (IOPSH) as of March 2020, further solidifying her role as a pivotal Member of the International Forum in support of the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals from April 2020. Princess Romona was also appointed to the position as Honorary Vice-President of the EUMMAS (European Marketing and Management Association) Women's Forum which is based in BosniaHerzegovina in 2021 based on her involvement in women's activities and leadership worldwide.

Her Highness's commitment to national representation was fortified by her appointment as RSG Commissioner for Heraldry and Sovereignty to Malaysia and as an Official Delegate by the Royal Society Group World, reflecting the depth of her international relations established since January 2014.

A trailblazer for women, Princess Romona holds the prestigious title of Asia President for the Chamber of Inter Commerce under the International Royal Academy of United Nations (IRAOUN), based in New York. As an Honorary Doctorate Member and the inaugural woman from the Far East in a senior role within the IRAOUN, she weaves a global platform of networks and partnerships.

Her humanitarian spirit shines as she engages with altruistic organizations, including her roles with Fight Cancer Global and Women Who Do Wonders International, and as an Ambassador for the Global Tolerance Faces, Dubai.

Recognition of her tireless humanitarian efforts was heralded by the Global Academy for Human Excellence (GAHE) on International Women's Day 2020 in Manila, bestowing upon her the accolade of Top Women Icon Global 2020. Her advocacy and unwavering dedication to peace, love, and human rights have earned her the affectionate title "Princess of Hearts." Due to her resolute commitment to peace and humanity, Princess Romona also being nominated for "The Medal of Excellence for the Most Influential Figures in the World 2014" by ICHAPS (International Council of Human Rights, Arbitration and Political Studies).

Furthering her commitment to global peace and development, Princess Romona was honoured as a recipient of the "Grand Order of the Noble Hand of Honor" by PALEDEC Africa in recognition of her outstanding virtues and selfless services in protecting the twelve rights of a child as contained in the United Nations Convention on the Rights of the Child, in Accra, Ghana on 7th Sept 2019. Her Highness also a Board of Advisor for Malaysia for Rockfield College of Sciences and Technology and a Non-Executive Board Member of the Organization for the Economic Reform of Lebanon (based in London, UK).

In her latest groundbreaking role, in 2024, she has been elected by the 360 Leadership Committee of the Marine Foundation to the Prominent First Ladies Club of the Royal Department, embracing a lifelong mandate to amplify the voices of women globally and advocate for the development of institutions and schools dedicated to the wellbeing of children worldwide. Princess Romona also holds position as the President for the Marine Foundation, Malaysia since 2024. Not to mention, Princess Romona has been appointed on 30th October 2024 as an Honorary Country Chairperson for the UNAccc Malaysia. Unity of Nations Action for Climate Change Councils is based in Gujerat, India and it is committed to protect Mother Earth from global warming, and other adverse impacts of climate change.

With her royal hand extended in service, Princess Dato' Seri Dr. Romona Murad continues to be a beacon of hope and an architect of change for communities across the globe.

Rina Telesphore, Dr, His Royal Highness, The Prince

Head of the Royal House of Andriakazomanga-Zafimbolamena MAZAVA

Official Biography of HRH Prince Dr. Rina Telesphore

His Royal Highness Prince Dr. Rina Telesphore exemplifies visionary leadership rooted in heritage and guided by a commitment to progress. As a descendant of King Radama I of Madagascar and King-Priest Andriakazomangabemanefoefo, he blends spiritual and temporal leadership to promote justice, peace, and sustainable development.

Advancing Economic Growth and Sustainability

HRH Prince Dr. Rina Telesphore is a prominent advocate for economic progress and international collaboration. As a Board Advisor to the Asian-African Chamber of Commerce and Industry (AACCI), he fosters partnerships between Asia and Africa, encouraging equitable trade and sustainable development. As President-Patron of Trade and Investment for the Commonwealth Entrepreneurs Club, he works to attract responsible investments, strengthen local enterprises, and ensure ethical practices. His contributions to the mining sector, as President of the Association of Jewelers, Lapidaries, and Mining Operators of Madagascar, underscore his dedication to sustainable resource management and community empowerment.

Promoting Diplomacy and Peacebuilding

HRH Prince Dr. Rina Telesphore is a strong proponent of global diplomacy and international cooperation. Serving as an Honorary Board Advisor for the World Peace and Diplomacy Organization (WPDO) and a World Peace Ambassador, he actively supports initiatives that resolve conflicts, enhance intercultural understanding, and build bridges between nations. His work reflects a steadfast commitment to justice, human dignity, and harmonious global relations.

Educational and Spiritual Leadership

HRH Prince Dr. Rina Telesphore's exceptional academic achievements include:

  • Professional Doctorate in Global Leadership and Management (European International University, Paris)
  • Doctorate in Business Administration in Banking (Harvest Christian University, USA)
  • Leadership Development Diploma (World Faith Bible Institute)
  • Master Jeweler Certification (HBJO-Paris)

Inheriting the spiritual legacy of King-Priest Andriakazomangabemanefoefo, he was anointed as Senior Pastor in 2012 within the Sanctuaire Chrétien International. This role enables him to integrate values of love, unity, and justice into his leadership, inspiring communities to work together for shared goals.

A Visionary Leader for the Future

As Head of the Royal House of Andriakazomanga-Zafimbolamena MAZAVA, HRH Prince Dr. Rina Telesphore embodies the balance of tradition and modernity. His leadership continues to inspire global efforts for justice, cultural preservation, and shared prosperity, shaping a future founded on unity and enduring harmony.

DR. HUZAIFA KHORAKIWALA

Chairman of the Summit

An MBA from Yale University, USA, Dr. Huzaifa Khorakiwala is the Executive Director of Wockhardt Limited and is part of the promoter family of the Wockhardt Group - a global pharmaceutical and biotechnology enterprise. The Wockhardt Group is India's research-based global healthcare enterprise with relevance in the fields of Pharmaceuticals, Biotechnology, and a chain of advanced Super Speciality Hospitals. It has 3 research centres and 7 manufacturing plants, with businesses ranging from the manufacture and marketing of Pharmaceutical and Bio-pharmaceutical formulations, Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs), and Vaccines. (www.wockhardt.com)

Dr. Khorakiwala is also the CEO of Wockhardt Foundation, a not-for-profit organization which runs several social service and human welfare programmes in health, education, water, and sanitation across the country, benefitting & touching millions of lives every year. The mission of Wockhardt Foundation is to work for the upliftment of the poor, weak, and needy. The organization is governed by a simple philosophy – "where every smile counts". (www.wockhardtfoundation.org)

Wockhardt Foundation's flagship healthcare initiative, Mobile 1000, provides doorstep delivery of free primary healthcare to people living in the remotest and rural parts of India. During the last financial year (2023-24), over 2.6 million patients benefitted from the 174 vans that were operating in 18 Indian states and 1 union territory, covering 2389 villages across India.

Dr. Khorakiwala is the Founder of "The World Peacekeepers Movement", an online movement comprising more than 2 million peacekeepers.

It is based on the 7 Peace Values of gratitude, forgiveness, love, humility, giving, patience, and truth.

Dr. Huzaifa Khorakiwala is Chairman, Global Justice, Love & Peace Summit, to be held in Dubai on 13 April, 2025. (www.justice-love-peace.com)

Dr. Khorakiwala is Chairman of Bahram Technologies & Services, a company involved in Mining & Solar Power. (www.bahramtechnologies.com)

He is also Chairman of Mushtaree Technologies, a digital health company.

For his contribution to society, he has received many prestigious awards (90 so far) & titles, including a Knighthood – which was bestowed on him by the Ecumenical Medical-Humanitarian Order Knights of St. John of Jerusalem (Knights of Charity). He is also the recipient of 15 Honorary Doctorates and 2 D.Litts.

Habil Khorakiwala, Dr.

Industrialist : Chairman, Wockhardt Group

An Industry Stalwart, Dr. Habil Khorakiwala has and continues to don numerous hats in his professional life of five decades while keeping his scientific temper of a young learner intact. An alumnus of Purdue University and Harvard Business School, Dr. Khorakiwala with his mantra of 'Vision to Execute', has successfully led Wockhardt to be a major Indian Pharmaceutical company that holds a strong presence in key global economies today. Amidst continuous business diversification, Corporate Social Responsibility has remained close to his heart for which he instituted Wockhardt Foundation committed to human welfare.

Dr. Khorakiwala has been widely acknowledged for his visionary approach in the industry and has served key positions as President of FICCI, IPA and served in various Govt. of India Forums. He has also as the Chancellor of Jamia Hamdard University in New Delhi and the Chairman of the Board of Governors at the Centre for Organisation & Development in Hyderabad, a non-profit scientific and industrial research organisation and a recognised doctoral research centre. He has been a member of World Economic Forum, and is an active Rotarian and member of Arch C. Klumph Society of Rotary International.

For one who lives such a fulfilling life, awards and accolades are no strangers and Dr. Khorakiwala isn't an exception here. His alma mater Purdue University, bestowed the prestigious title of 'Distinguished Alumnus' for significant contribution to the Profession of Pharmacy and honoured him with their highest recognition, the 'Honorary Doctorate' in 2010. He is the only non-American and the first Indian in 125 year history of Pharmacy School of the University to be awarded the Honorary Doctorate. He has received the Shiromani Vikas Award for his 'Outstanding and Inspiring Contribution towards National Development' at the hands of Mother Teresa (1992); He was awarded – the 'Entrepreneur of the Year' award by Ernst and Young (2004) and again by UK Trade and Investment (2008); 'Lifetime Achievement Award' at Pharma Excellence Awards by Indian Express Group (2006) and by Frost and Sullivan (2012). Received the 'Global Indian of the Year 2018-19' award from AsiaOne Magazine & URS Media Consulting PL.

In 2017, Dr. Khorakiwala authored 'Odyssey of Courage', a book chronicling his entrepreneurial journey, and in 2018, he established the Wockhardt School of Courage, a unique mentorship programme for young and budding entrepreneurs, which is based on tenets, principles, and insights drawn from the book.

Dr. Khorakiwala is still personally involved in every major scientific endeavour at Wockhardt, and dreams to see India as a global leader in both healthcare manufacturing and research and development.

Binod K. Chaudhary

Chairman Of Chaudhary Group & CG Corp Global

Dr. Binod Chaudhary is the visionary Chairman of Chaudhary Group & CG Corp Global (CG), Nepal's largest and most dynamic multinational conglomerate. CG Corp Global, a multidimensional enterprise, operates a diverse portfolio of 202 companies and 261 brands across five continents and 32 countries, employing more than 20,000 people with a net worth of $1.8 billion.

Widely regarded as one of Nepal's most innovative business leaders, Dr. Chaudhary has been instrumental in transforming his family business into a global powerhouse. CG's operations span numerous sectors, including Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG), hospitality, education, financial services, telecommunications, electronics, energy, cement, and real estate, amongst others.

Among CG's many successes, Dr. Chaudhary is best known as the "Noodle King" for his pioneering role in the global success of Wai Wai noodles, which holds over 2.6% of the global instant noodle market. He also plays a vital role in the financial sector through ownership of Nabil Bank, Nepal's largest bank in the private sector, and Union Bank of Colombo in Sri Lanka.

Dr. Chaudhary made his beginning in the hospitality space back in 1999 by partnering with the Tata Groups' IHCL and his journey in the hospitality space now spans over 25 years, across 185+ hotels in 12 countries with almost 10,000 rooms, reflecting his commitment to understanding evolving markets and with a vision to add 25 hotels each year, to reach 200+ hotels by 2025.

Dr. Chaudhary's leadership extends beyond business into philanthropy and public service. He founded the Chaudhary Foundation in 1995, through which he has championed sustainable development, social responsibility, and disaster relief. The Foundation has played a critical role in healthcare, education, and community development in Nepal, notably leading rebuilding efforts after the 2015 earthquake. His foundation's contributions to sustainable business practices and improving the lives of Nepali people are well-recognized globally.

Dr. Chaudhary is also an influential figure in Nepal's political transformation and economic spheres. He is a respected member of the Nepali Congress Central Working Committee and has served as an Honorable Member of Parliament since 2018. He previously held a seat in Nepal's Constituent Assembly from 2008 to 2012, contributing to the drafting of Nepal's constitution. Additionally, he serves as President Emeritus of the Confederation of Nepalese Industries (CNI). His extensive contributions to business include serving as the former President of the Federation of Nepalese Chambers of Commerce & Industry (FNCCI), Nepal's foremost business organization.

On the global stage, Dr. Chaudhary is an active member of the World Presidents' Organization (WPO/YPO), where he engages with other world leaders to promote business excellence. In addition to his professional and philanthropic endeavors, Dr. Chaudhary is an avid trekker, fitness enthusiast, and a strong advocate of maintaining a balanced and healthy lifestyle. This passion complements his dynamic leadership style, which continues to inspire many in Nepal and beyond. Dr. Chaudhary has also published multiple books including his best-selling autobiography, 'Making it Big'. Chaudhary's drive and dedication are his inherent strengths. And contrary to popular belief, he proved an entrepreneur can do business in Nepal and still emerge as a billionaire — the only one from the Himalayan republic listed by Forbes. His extraordinary journey from a modest family venture to global success is a testament to his entrepreneurial spirit, relentless innovation, and deep commitment to both business and society.

Dr. Satpal Singh was Knighted by the Government of Philippines and given the title of "Sir". He has received a doctorate degree (Phd) in Sikh Theology. He was appointed a Deputy Sherrif in New Mexico and has received many honors and awards from the USA House of Congress, the USA Senate and various other accolades from India and various countries. These awards and titles represent Dr. Satpal Singh's selfless service to the global community.

He is a Peace Ambassador for various global interfaith organizations including several United Nations affiliated organizations. He is an outspoken individual who supports efforts to control climate and ecological damage to the environment and bring awareness to action plans to save the Earth and Humanity.

In his work with the global interfaith communities, he has spent thousands of hours in "Seva", Selfless Volunteer Service, in many parts of the world helping with free food (Langar-a Sikh tradition), medical camps, sponsoring students who cannot afford school and college fees and helping the under-privileged in Asia and India.

Sir Satpal Singh travels extensively around the world participating in peace and interfaith events which promote harmony and goodwill in the society. During his attendance at the events, he shares the teachings of the Sikh faith which promotes Oneness of Humanity to bring unity and love in the world.

He did his Bachelors Degree in Science from the prestigious St. Stephen's College at Delhi University, India and migrated to USA in 1978. During his initial years in the USA, he has been involved in various different businesses, including food manufacturing, international trade and real estate development and investments. Now, he mostly devotes his time to religious, spiritual and life changing technologies and uplifting the human fraternity.

He is a lifestyle coach and teaches a Healthy Happy Holy lifestyle with natural remedies, Yoga & Meditation and Science of Humanology which aims to promote successful realization of our human potential in its excellence through the study of spiritual and human nature. He shares the technology of utilizing Mind, Body and Soul for optimum Self Realization.

His teaching sessions and classes guide the participants to the many varied techniques and experience of the universal energy within each of us. He often lectures to young students and at various schools and universities in USA and internationally.

His mission is to better facilitate the current worldwide shift in global consciousness from instability, extremism, conflicts and lack of empathy, to harmony, cooperation and enduring peace in the world.

He lives in Los Angeles, California and in New Mexico, USA with his wife and two daughters.

Bhai Sahib Satpal Singh Khalsa
