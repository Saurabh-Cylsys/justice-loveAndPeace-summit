Privacy Policy

1

Introduction

Welcome to justice-love-peace.com! This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal data when you interact with our services, website, or events. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy in compliance with:

  • Global Privacy Standards (e.g., GDPR, UK Data Protection Act).
  • UAE Federal Law No. 45 of 2021 on the Protection of Personal Data (PDPL).

By using our website or participating in our services, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2

Scope of the Policy

This Policy applies to:

  • Individuals accessing our website or digital platforms.
  • Guests, participants, or speakers at our events.
  • Business associates, vendors, or partners interacting with us.

It governs all data collection channels, including in-person interactions, digital communications, third-party integrations, and our mobile applications.

3

Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email, phone number, address, nationality, and job title.
  • Sensitive Personal Data: Health details, dietary preferences, and accessibility requirements (only with your explicit consent).
  • Event-Specific Information: Registration details, participation preferences, and media content.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser types, device details, and activity logs.
  • Payment Information: Credit card details or other financial data.
  • Multimedia Content: Photos, videos, and recordings from events.
  • Cookies and Tracking Information: Behavioral data collected through cookies and similar technologies.
4

Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data based on:

  • Consent: For specific processing activities like marketing or sensitive data handling.
  • Contractual Obligations: To fulfill services you request, such as event participation or booking.
  • Legal Compliance: To adhere to UAE and international regulatory requirements.
  • Legitimate Interests: For purposes like improving user experience, ensuring security, and fraud prevention.
5

How We Use Your Data

Your data will be used to:

  • Register and manage your participation in events or services.
  • Provide tailored experiences based on your preferences.
  • Send important updates or respond to your inquiries.
  • Meet legal and regulatory obligations.
  • Ensure event security and logistics.
  • Market events or services, with your consent.
6

Sharing and Disclosure of Data

Your personal data may be shared with:

  • Service Providers: For operations such as event registration, logistics, and accommodations.
  • Event Partners and Sponsors: For networking and marketing purposes (only with your prior consent).
  • Government Authorities: As required by UAE or international law for compliance or lawful requests.
  • Third-Party Processors: For data storage, analytics, and service delivery, under strict confidentiality agreements.

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

7

International Data Transfers

If your personal data is transferred outside the UAE, we ensure it is protected through:

  • Adequacy Decisions: Complying with regions recognized for adequate privacy safeguards.
  • Data Transfer Agreements: Incorporating Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent safeguards.
8

Data Retention

If your personal data is transferred outside the UAE, we ensure it is protected through:

  • We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined or as required by law. Media content (e.g., photos and videos) may be stored indefinitely for promotional purposes unless you request its removal.
9

Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption protocols.
  • Secure storage facilities.
  • Regular audits and access controls.

While no system is entirely secure, we take reasonable precautions to mitigate risks.

10

Your Rights

Under applicable laws, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request details of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of data under specific circumstances.
  • Restriction: Limit the processing of your personal data.
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke previously given consent without affecting the lawfulness of past processing.

To exercise your rights, contact us at help@justice-love-peace.com.

11

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to:

  • Enhance your browsing experience.
  • Collect analytics to improve our services.
  • Provide targeted advertisements (with your consent).

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings or our cookie management tool.

12

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a minor has provided data, contact us immediately for deletion.

Privacy Provisions for Speakers and Delegates

1. Types of Data Collected from Speakers and Delegates As a speaker or delegate at our events, we may collect and process the following categories of personal data:

  • Personal Identification Information:
    • Name, job title, organization, contact details (email, phone, address), nationality, and professional biography.
  • Event-Specific Information:
    • Presentation topics, session details, and event agenda participation.
    • Dietary preferences, accessibility needs, or special requests.
  • Media Content:
    • Photos, videos, or audio recordings during sessions, panels, or networking activities.
    • Any content shared during presentations, such as slides, documents, or multimedia.
  • Travel and Accommodation Information:
    • Passport or visa details (if assistance is provided for international travel).
    • Hotel bookings or transportation arrangements facilitated by us.
  • Social Media or Publicly Available Information:
    • Public profiles or content shared for promotional purposes related to the event.
  • Consent Forms:
    • Agreements for the recording and use of personal content during the event.

2. How Speaker and Delegate Data is Used Data collected from speakers and delegates may be used for the following purposes:

  • Event Management:
    • Scheduling and coordination of event sessions, panels, or keynotes.
    • Communicating important updates and logistical information.
  • Marketing and Promotion:
    • Featuring speaker profiles, bios, and session details on event websites, brochures, and marketing materials.
    • Sharing event highlights, including recordings and photos, on social media and other promotional channels.
  • Networking Facilitation:
    • Enabling connections between speakers, delegates, and attendees through dedicated platforms or apps.
    • Sharing limited information (e.g., name, organization) with other participants for networking purposes.
  • Compliance:
    • Ensuring compliance with legal and regulatory obligations, such as visa processing or health and safety requirements.
  • Feedback and Improvement:
    • Collecting post-event feedback to improve future events.

3. Sharing of Speaker and Delegate Data We may share speaker and delegate information with:

  • Event Partners and Corporate Partners:
    • To coordinate sponsored sessions, co-branded activities, or networking engagements.
    • Sharing limited delegate details (e.g., job title, organization) where consent is provided.
  • Service Providers:
    • For logistical arrangements, such as venue coordination, travel bookings, or AV support.
  • Government Authorities:
    • For visa processing, tax compliance, or other regulatory requirements.

4. Media and Publicity

  • Event Recordings and Live Streams:
    • Sessions and presentations may be recorded or live-streamed for promotional or educational purposes.
    • By participating as a speaker or delegate, you grant us the right to use such recordings, unless explicitly stated otherwise in a written request.
  • Promotional Content:
    • Your name, bio, and session details may be included in event promotions, both online and offline.
  • Opt-Out Options:
    • Delegates and speakers may opt-out of specific promotional uses by contacting help@justice-love-peace.com.

5. Consent Management for Speakers and Delegates Speakers and delegates may be asked to provide explicit consent for:

  • The use of their name, photo, and session materials in marketing collateral
  • Inclusion in networking tools or apps provided to event participants.
  • The collection of sensitive data, such as dietary or accessibility requirements.

6. Your Rights as Speakers and Delegates Speakers and delegates retain the following rights regarding their personal data:

  • Access and Portability:
    • Request a copy of your data or details on how it is being processed.
  • Data Correction:
    • Update inaccuracies in your profile or session details.
  • Withdraw Consent:
    • Revoke consent for promotional use of recordings or materials (note that past usage cannot be undone).
  • Erasure Requests:
    • Request removal of specific content, such as photos or videos, from our platforms.
  • Restrict Processing:
    • Limit data processing for non-essential activities, such as marketing.

7. Retention of Speaker and Delegate Data

  • Retention Periods:
    • Data related to event coordination (e.g., session materials, registration details) may be retained indefinitely for further references.
    • Media content (e.g., recordings, photos) may be retained indefinitely for promotional or archival purposes unless a removal request is submitted.
  • Data Deletion Requests:
    • To request the deletion of specific data, please contact help@justice-love-peace.com.

8. Speaker and Delegate Networking Tools For networking purposes, we may use platforms or apps that:

  • Allow participants to connect and share professional details.
  • Require opt-in consent for visibility in networking features.
    • Participants can manage their privacy preferences through the platform settings or by contacting us directly.

  • Event Optimization:
    • We may use artificial intelligence (AI) and advanced analytics tools to analyze speaker and delegate data for personalized. recommendations, session optimization, and attendee engagement strategies.
